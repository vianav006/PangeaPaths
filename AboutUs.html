ABOUT US
At Pangea Paths, we're a team of current students and alumni who have personally faced and overcome the challenges of navigating the U.S. job market as international students. Our unique experiences with visa requirements and employment processes, including F-1, OPT, CPT, and H-1B, have equipped us with invaluable insights. We've created this platform to extend dedicated support and guidance to international students at UNH aiming for success in Corporate America.

WHO WE ARE

MISSION

Our mission at Pangea Paths is to empower international students with the tools, knowledge, and support necessary to navigate the complexities of the U.S. job market. By offering tailored advice, curated employer lists, and up-to-date immigration insights, we aim to prepare students for success in Corporate America while advocating for equitable career opportunities.

VISION

We envision a future where international students effortlessly join the U.S. workforce, equipped with the necessary confidence and skills for their careers. Pangea Paths aims to be the bridge connecting diverse talents with inclusive opportunities, fostering a dynamic and equitable professional environment.

CORE VALUES

We are guided by a commitment to personalized support, drawing on our direct experiences with F-1, OPT, CPT, and H-1B processes to inform our approach. Our dedication to inclusivity and accessibility underpins our efforts, as we strive to ensure every international student can achieve success in the U.S. career landscape.
